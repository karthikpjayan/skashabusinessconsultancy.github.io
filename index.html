PAINTS AND COATINGS

Our paints and coating products are quickly and simply applied internal finish for walls and ceilings designed to give a finish which is ready for painting. The coatings are based on European technology , eco-friendly, non hazardous, non toxic, strongly recommended for any surfaces where water based coatings are acceptable to safeguard the interests of environment and society. Our coatings can be the best finishing system of its kind in India with an unsurpassed reputation for case of use, speed of application and quality of finish.

The Coatings are formed with raw materials related to natural resources and in accordance with European standards. Our coatings can be used as a thick or thin coat product, a cost effective alternative to traditional painting system or plaster on concrete finish or as a textured finishing coat for internal walls and ceilings.

Product Range
